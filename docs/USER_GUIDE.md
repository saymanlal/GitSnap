# 📘 GitSnap User Guide

## Welcome to GitSnap!

GitSnap is a **completely free** image cloud that lets you store, share, and manage your photos without any subscription fees. No credit card required, no hidden costs, just unlimited storage.

## Quick Start (30 seconds)

### Sign Up
1. Visit your GitSnap URL (e.g., `https://gitsnap-cloud.vercel.app`)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top navigation
3. Enter your **First Name** and **Last Name**
4. Click **"Sign Up"**
5. **Your secret key file will download automatically** - SAVE THIS FILE! It's your only way to access your account.

### First Upload
1. You'll be automatically logged in and taken to your dashboard
2. Drag & drop images onto the upload area (or click to select)
3. Click **"Upload All Images"**
4. Your photos appear in the gallery instantly!

## Managing Your Account

### Signing In (On Any Device)

1. Go to GitSnap and click **"Sign In"**
2. Enter your **Full Name** (exactly as you registered)
3. Click **"Choose File"** and select your `.key` file
4. Click **"Sign In"**
5. You're in! All your photos are there.

**⚠️ Important:** Your secret key file is the ONLY way to access your account. If you lose it, there's no way to recover your images. Keep it safe!

### Signing Out

Click **"Sign Out"** in the top right corner of any page.

## Using the Dashboard

### Uploading Images

- **Drag & Drop:** Drag images from your computer onto the upload area
- **Click to Select:** Click the upload area and choose files
- **Multiple Files:** Hold Ctrl (Windows) or Cmd (Mac) to select multiple images

**Supported formats:** JPEG, PNG, WEBP, GIF  
**Max size per image:** 10MB  
**No limit on total images!**

### Queue System

- Images you select go into a "pending queue"
- You can add more images before uploading
- Click **"Clear Queue"** to remove pending images
- Click **"Upload All Images"** to upload everything at once

### Viewing Gallery

- Images appear automatically after upload
- Scroll down to lazy-load more images
- Gallery updates in real-time

### Downloading Images

- Click the **⬇️ (download)** button on any image
- Image downloads in **original quality** (no compression!)

### Deleting Images

- Click the **🗑️ (delete)** button on any image
- Confirm deletion
- Image is permanently removed from the cloud

### Refreshing Gallery

- Click **"⟳ Refresh"** to reload your gallery
- Useful if images don't appear immediately after upload

## Tips & Best Practices

### ✅ Do This:
- **Save your secret key file** in multiple safe places (USB drive, cloud storage, printed copy)
- **Upload in batches** (10-20 images at once for best performance)
- **Use descriptive filenames** (e.g., "summer2024_beach.jpg")
- **Log out** on shared computers
- **Test your key file** by signing in on different browsers

### ❌ Don't Do This:
- **Don't lose your key file** - it cannot be recovered!
- **Don't share your key file** - anyone with it can access your photos
- **Don't upload copyrighted content** - you're responsible for your images
- **Don't delete your only copy** of important photos

## Troubleshooting

### "Failed to load gallery"
**Solution:** Refresh the page. If persists, sign out and sign in again.

### "Invalid credentials during sign in"
**Solution:** Make sure you're entering your full name exactly as registered. Check that you're uploading the correct `.key` file.

### Upload seems stuck
**Solution:** Check your internet connection. Refresh the page - your images should still be there.

### Images aren't downloading
**Solution:** Check browser popup blockers. Try right-click → "Save image as" instead.

### Can't sign up
**Solution:** Ensure both first and last name are filled. Try a different browser.

### Gallery is slow
**Solution:** Clear your browser cache. Use the refresh button to reload only new images.

## Security & Privacy

### How GitSnap Protects You

- **No passwords** - Nothing to hack or steal
- **Secret key encryption** - Your key is cryptographically secure
- **HTTPS only** - All traffic is encrypted
- **No tracking** - We don't collect analytics
- **Your data, your control** - Delete images anytime

### Recommended Security Practices

1. **Store your key file in encrypted storage** (Bitwarden, 1Password, etc.)
2. **Use different key files for different devices** (sign up multiple times)
3. **Regularly delete old/unused images**
4. **Don't use public computers** to access your account
5. **Create a new key file** every 6 months (sign up again, move images)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is it really free forever?
**Yes!** GitSnap costs $0 to run and will always be free for personal use.

### How many images can I store?
**Unlimited!** Thanks to GitHub's free tier, you can store as many images as you want.

### Can I share my images with others?
**Yes!** Right-click any image and "Copy image address" to share the direct link.

### What if I lose my secret key file?
**Unfortunately, your account cannot be recovered.** That's why it's crucial to save backups of your key file. We recommend storing it in multiple safe locations.

### Can I use GitSnap on my phone?
**Yes!** GitSnap is fully responsive and works great on mobile. You can also "Install" it as a PWA (Progressive Web App) - look for "Add to Home Screen" in your browser menu.

### Are my images private?
**Yes!** Your images are stored in a private GitHub repository. Only you (and anyone with your key file) can access them.

### Can I organize images into folders?
**Coming soon!** Currently, images are sorted by upload date.

### What image formats are supported?
JPEG, PNG, WEBP, and GIF.

### Is there a file size limit?
10MB per image (for performance). GitHub's actual limit is 100MB but we set 10MB for faster uploads.
